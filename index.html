Powerful and flexible features for your content

Workflow

Our simple, flexible and powerful workflow system puts you in control of your content. Move through the editorial stages of draft, review, edit, and approval to get your content ready for publishing. You and your team are updated so everyone knows what they need to be working on. All activity is tracked for transparency and accountability.

Feedback

Tracking changes in a word document is collaborative writing, not a review. CS Workflow makes the content read-only and only allows for comments during review. This makes the process faster for the reviewer, because they can focus on their area of expertise rather than the copywriting. The comments are correlated as a todo list, giving editors actionable feedback.

Content types

Extend your content beyond the main body with our customisable content types. Content types allow you to add editable and reviewable attributes to your content, e.g. add a subject to an email or meta-description to a webpage. Content type attributes can be used by integrations and preview templates in advanced setups. All our plans allow for unlimited content types.

Integrations

Publish your content directly from CS Workflow to Wordpress and Twitter. You can use content types for more flexible publishing options. For example set custom values for author, category, and title when you publish to Wordpress. The platforms we integrate with are one of the main areas we will expand.

Preview templates

Receiving an out of context block of content can make it difficult for reviewers and approvers to picture how the audience will see your content. Our HTML and CSS based preview templates make it easy to see your content in context. With one click you can import pages from your website or blog to make preview templates. Check out our video about how preview templates are setup and used.

Online

There's no software to download and install. Your team and workflow can be setup quickly without the hassle of dealing with different computers and their software compatibilities. Your content is securely stored online for your team to access from any device at any time.

Better than email by design

Almost all of us use email to send content for review and approval. A shared folder system is often used for storing content in document form. This is easy and cheap, but comes with a lot of problems and risks.

Email does not have any workflow, approval support or version control. These are vital to ensuring reviews and publishing risks are properly managed. CS Workflow has these features at its core, making it the best tool for your content review process.

What are workspaces?

Workspaces are secure content areas that support a single team of content managers, writers, reviewers, and approvers. If you work with clients then you'll need one workspace per client.

Can I support different clients in one workspace?

You wouldn't want to. Like a shared folder, content can be read by anyone who is a member of the workspace.

Will I be locked into a long contract?

The Team and Agency plans are month-to-month and you can cancel any time.

What happens after my free trial?

If you want to continue using CS Workflow at the end of the 30 days free trial then you simply activate your account using our secure credit card payment facility.

Are there any hidden fees or restrictions?

The monthly price you pay for your plan covers everything. There are no limits on users or content so you can grow your content team without us getting in the way or costing you more.
